TempoBoard on Tempo

TempoBoard is a pathUSD-native meme launchpad built on Tempo Mainnet. The product pairs Tempo's payments-first design with internal curve trading, a clear graduation threshold, and a defined post-curve liquidity path.

1. Chain

TempoBoard runs on Tempo Mainnet because the launch flow is expressed in stablecoin terms from start to finish. Token creation, price discovery, and graduation are all designed around a single chain and a single settlement asset.

Network Parameters

Network: Tempo Mainnet
Chain ID: 4217
RPC: https://rpc.tempo.xyz

Settlement Asset

All current platform trading is settled in pathUSD on Tempo, which keeps launch pricing, trade flow, and graduation accounting in stablecoin terms.
pathUSD: 0x20c0000000000000000000000000000000000000

  • Users need an EVM wallet connected to Tempo Mainnet.
  • Token creation, buying, and selling all run on Tempo without switching to a separate execution environment.
  • The current launch flow uses pathUSD as the settlement token for the internal curve.
  • This makes the entire launch experience easier to reason about in USD terms instead of volatile gas-asset terms.

2. Price Curve and External Migration

TempoBoard uses a quadratic internal curve for price discovery. Trading stays inside the platform until 80% of supply is sold, and only then does the project graduate toward external liquidity deployment.

Primary Launch Contract

0x5866914946e4B7c7B2C789cc5EE259b73CBa2274

Internal Sale Cap

800,000,000 tokens
This represents 80% of the total supply.

Curve Formula
price(sold) = 0.000003 + 0.000062 * (sold / 800,000,000)^2
Detailed Calculation
Let: saleCap = 800,000,000 r = sold / saleCap Then: price(sold) = 0.000003 + 0.000062 * r^2 Buy side: buyFee = pathUsdIn * 1% netPathUsd = pathUsdIn - buyFee tokensOut = netPathUsd / price(sold) Sell side: grossPathUsdBack = tokensIn * price(sold) sellFee = grossPathUsdBack * 1% pathUsdOut = grossPathUsdBack - sellFee Migration condition: migrate when sold >= 800,000,000
  • The curve starts near 0.00000300 pathUSD.
  • Because the pricing model is quadratic, the price accelerates more aggressively later in the sale than at the beginning.
  • The simplified ratio form is r = sold / 800,000,000, so all price movement is driven by sold supply progress on the internal curve.
  • For buys, the 1% fee is removed first, then the remaining pathUSD is converted at the current curve price.
  • For sells, the token amount is first converted back into gross pathUSD at the current curve price, then the 1% fee is deducted.
  • External migration is triggered only after 800,000,000 tokens are sold on the internal curve.
  • On a no-sell path, completely filling the curve is about 19,124.58 pathUSD gross user spend.
  • After the current 1% trading fee, the contract retains about 18,933.33 pathUSD net.
  • At graduation, the remaining 20% of token supply and 18,933.33 pathUSD are intended to be added as liquidity on Uniswap V2.

3. Fee Policy

TempoBoard does not use an official platform token in the current fee model. The protocol applies a 1% trading tax and routes that fee into token buybacks, partner distribution, and staking rewards.

Buyback + Burn

50% of the 1% trading tax is used to buy back and burn the best-performing token from the past 24h.

Partner Allocation

20% of the 1% trading tax is used to buy back the best-performing token from the past 24h for partner distribution.

Staking Allocation

30% of the 1% trading tax is allocated to staking rewards and distributed every 24h.

Reward Formula

reward(i, e) = stakingPool(e) * stake(i, e) / totalStaked(e)

24h Staking Reward Formula
For each reward epoch e: taxPool(e) = totalTradingVolume(e) * 1% stakingPool(e) = taxPool(e) * 30% For staker i: reward(i, e) = stakingPool(e) * stake(i, e) / totalStaked(e)
  • 50% of fee value is used to buy back and burn the strongest token on the board over the past 24 hours.
  • 20% of fee value is used to buy back the strongest token on the board over the past 24 hours for partner distribution.
  • 30% of the fee pool is distributed to stakers every 24 hours, proportional to stake share.
